Collectorate School School & College has a rich library containing about different books, Besides, syllabus boos it has a large collection of books on liberation wars, Science, Games & Sports, Religious books, Novels, General Knowledge, Fictions, Magazine, Journals, Daily Newspapers, History etc. to enrich the knowledge of young learners.
