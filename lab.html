Computer Lab:
Keeping in mind the vision 2021 declared by Bangladesh Government to build up a digital Bangladesh, Collectorate School & College, Rangpur is going to make CSCR digital for thr students to gate the taste of digital world.
CSCR comprises the largest Computer Lab situated at the forth floor of the newly constructed Six storied gorgeous building where students as well as teachers browse internet to get acquainted with the rest of the undiscovered world of knowledge. Students can peruse books through internet. The students also perform their practical classes.
The lab is the most enriched and gorgeously decorated consisting of more than 40 computers having latest technology. Computer Education is taught here through audio-visual method with the help of Multimedia.

PHYSICS LABORATORY:
Collectorate School & College, Rangpur has rich Physics Lab. This lab gives students an in-depth experience in experimental physics so that they can understand how laboratory measurements test concepts in physics. Support facilities make it possible to extend and modify the individual experiments. From literature references students are expected to design their experiments, assemble and perhaps partially construct the apparatus, and report on the results in the form of a technical paper.

CHEMISTRY LABORATORY:
Chemistry is one of the crucial subjects of CSCR located in the hub of Rangpur, a newly formed division by the government of Bangladesh. Department of chemistry has been with the college since the very outset of the college in 1996. This department comprises 5 experienced teachers, 1(one) experienced demonstrator, 1(one) lab Assistant and a peon working here. Students have facility to have access to the laboratory for practical classes. Besides, the necessary ingredients for helping students in classroom activities are supplied from the lab.
Department of chemistry plays a vital role in supplying necessary research, information and theory to fill up the thirst for knowledge of the inquisitive students for Science club.
